    Stretch

  • Stretching exercises encourage lengthening of your muscles and their associated tendons. They counteract the shortening and tightening of muscles that can occur immediately after vigorous exercise, and as a product of ageing and inactivity. A sedentary lifestyle that involves long periods of sitting or driving can cause your muscles to shorten and tighten. This reduces your range of motion and can lead to stiffness and pain.
